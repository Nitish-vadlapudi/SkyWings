Airline Online Check-In Portal – Enterprise Level Project Prompt

Design and develop a complete, modern, enterprise-grade Airline Online Check-In Portal that simulates a real-world airline customer experience. The platform should allow passengers to manage their journey from booking verification to boarding pass generation through a seamless digital workflow.

The website must be visually impressive, highly interactive, mobile-responsive, secure, scalable, and built with modern web development standards. The design should resemble premium airline platforms such as Emirates, Qatar Airways, Singapore Airlines, Lufthansa, or Air India.

The system must include separate Passenger and Admin portals with role-based authentication and authorization.

Core Objective

The primary objective of the platform is to provide passengers with a complete online check-in experience where they can:

Verify their booking using PNR or ticket number.
Complete the check-in process.
Select or upgrade seats.
Purchase additional baggage.
Choose meal preferences.
Manage passenger information.
Receive notifications and travel updates.
Generate and download a boarding pass with a QR code.
View travel history and loyalty rewards.

The system should eliminate the need for airport counter check-in for most passengers.
